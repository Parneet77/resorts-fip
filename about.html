Our History

Our resort's humble beginnings in 1995

Founded in 1995, Couples Resort began as a small beachfront property with a vision to create a sanctuary for couples seeking romance and relaxation. Over the years, we've grown into a world-class destination while maintaining our commitment to intimacy and personalized service.
