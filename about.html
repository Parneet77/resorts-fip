Our History

The original resort building from 1995

Founded in 1995, Couples Resort began as a small boutique hotel with just 10 rooms. Over the years, we've grown into a world-class destination while maintaining our commitment to providing intimate, personalized experiences for couples.

Our founders, John and Sarah Thompson, envisioned a place where couples could escape the everyday and reconnect in a setting of natural beauty and luxury. Today, their vision lives on through our dedicated staff and carefully curated experiences.
